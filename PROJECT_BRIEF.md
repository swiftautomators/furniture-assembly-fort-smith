# Furniture Assembly Fort Smith - Project Brief

## Business Overview

**Business Name:** Furniture Assembly Fort Smith  
**Domain:** furnitureassemblyfortsmith.com  
**Service Area:** Fort Smith, Arkansas and the Arkansas River Valley  
**Business Type:** Local furniture assembly specialist

## Target Market

### Primary Audience
- Homeowners and renters in Fort Smith and Arkansas River Valley
- Recent purchasers of RTA furniture from IKEA, Wayfair, Amazon, Target, Walmart
- Age: 25-45 years old
- Income: $40,000-$80,000 household income

### Secondary Audience
- Small businesses setting up offices
- Property managers furnishing rental units
- Interior designers needing assembly for clients

## Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

"Fort Smith's only dedicated furniture assembly specialist. Transparent flat-rate pricing. Local reliability. 100% satisfaction guaranteed."

## Competitive Advantage

1. **Exact Match Domain** - furnitureassemblyfortsmith.com for SEO
2. **Only Dedicated Specialist** - Not a generalist handyman
3. **Transparent Pricing** - Flat-rate tiers, no hidden fees
4. **Local Reliability** - Community-focused, not a national platform
5. **Market Gap** - No current specialized local competitor

## Pricing Structure

### Flat-Rate Tiers
- **Tier 1 (Simple):** $110-$130 - Nightstands, small desks, chairs
- **Tier 2 (Medium):** $150-$200 - Dressers, beds, dining tables
- **Tier 3 (Complex):** $225-$300+ - Wardrobes, bunk beds, office systems

### Additional Services
- Minimum Service Fee: $85
- Hourly Rate (Custom): $90/hour
- Premium Package: +$25-$50 (packaging removal, wall anchoring)
- Bulk Discount: 10-15% off for 5+ items
