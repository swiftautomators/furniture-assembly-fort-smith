After assembling hundreds of furniture pieces for families throughout Fort Smith and the Arkansas River Valley, I've learned a lot about what works, what doesn't, and what homeowners need to know. This blog is my way of giving back to our community by sharing that knowledge.

Over the coming weeks and months, we'll be publishing helpful articles covering topics like:

- **Assembly Tips & Tricks** - Professional techniques to make furniture assembly easier and faster

- **DIY vs. Professional** - When to tackle it yourself and when to call in the experts
